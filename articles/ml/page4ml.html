MOBILE LEGENDS

MPL-PHILIPPINES SEASON 7

MOBILE LEGENDS: BANG BANG PROFESSIONAL LEAGUE is the largest and most prestigious mobile games competition in Southeast Asia.

In the Philippines, the league was officially established in 2018 and Aether Main was the Season 1 champion.

Season 2 ran from October 2018 to January 2019 and saw Cignal Ultra defy the odds to win the title.

Season 3 was held between March and May 2019. This time, ArkAngel emerged as victors.

Season 4 saw Sunsparks emerge victorious, after a very back and forth grand finals.

In Season 5, Sunsparks won the champions again.

In Season 6, Bren Esports came out on top after a very impressive dominant run throughout the entirety of the season.

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Professional League PH Season 7 (MPL-PH S7) is now starting and it covers a qualifier stage, a Regular Season and a Playoffs event where the 10 teams will battle for a share of the $120,000 USD prize pool.
