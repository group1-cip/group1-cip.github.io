GENSHIN IMPACT SERENITEA POT SYSTEM

Greetings, Travelers~ The version update has been completed, and here are some sizzling tips for the basics on using the Serenitea Pot~




HOW TO ENTER/LEAVE THE SERENITEA POT


MTo enter the Serenitea Pot, you will need to first go to Inventory > Gadgets and select it to place it. After placing the Serenitea Pot, select Enter the Serenitea Pot and you will find yourself in the realm within. After entering the Serenitea Pot, the game will enter into Co-Op Mode. (Friends in the Serenitea Pot are unable to receive Domain Team Invites~)

To leave the Serenitea Pot, you can similarly place it from Inventory > Gadgets and interact with it to leave the Serenitea Pot.

In addition, you can open the Map, switch to the Teyvat world map by clicking on the bottom-right corner, and click on any Teleport Waypoint or Statue of The Seven to leave the Serenitea Pot. Upon leaving your friend's Serenitea Pot, you will return to Teyvat in your own world.

After entering the Serenitea Pot, click on the furnishings icon in the top-right corner to start decorating your realm.









HOW TO VISIT FRIENDS' SERENITEA POT

Travelers can visit the Serenitea Pot of friends of any World Level. Simply select a friend's avatar to request to visit their Serenitea Pot. You must add the other player as a friend before you can visit their Serenitea Pot~


You can click to open the permissions settings of the Serenitea Pot on the map interface.

If your friend has a furnishing that is in the process of being crafted, interacting with the teapot spirit in their Serenitea Pot will help to speed up the furnishing's crafting progress. This helps your friend reduce the creation time for furnishings by a certain amount. You can provide assistance to multiple friends each day, but the same person cannot be assisted twice.



Other than Guest Assistance, you may also use a Vial of Adeptal Speed to immediately obtain the furnishing currently being crafted. You can exchange for the Vial of Adeptal Speed at the Realm Depot.






HOW TO COLLECT WOOD

A new crafting function has been added to the Serenitea Pot system. Wood is one of the most important materials for crafting furnishings. Travelers can cut down trees in Teyvat to obtain a certain quantity of wood. This wood will be added to the Inventory automatically without having to be picked up.






HOW TO SWAP MATERIALS NEEDED FOR DYES

Paimon was just about to create some dyes when Paimon realized the Sunsettias needed to make the red dyes had been all eaten up! On the way to go gather more, Paimon heard that materials needed for dyes could be swapped with another. It turns out that Sunsettias needed for red dyes could be swapped with carrots, which Paimon has a ton of! This discovery saves a lot of time spent on gathering materials, so Paimon just had to share this tip with Travelers~






And that's all for this round of tips! We will be back to share more useful knowledge about the Serenitea Pot with everyone soon~
