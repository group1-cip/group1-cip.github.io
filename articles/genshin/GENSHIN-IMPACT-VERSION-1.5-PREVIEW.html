GENSHIN IMPACT VERSION 1.5 PREVIEW

Are you having trouble keeping up to date with the frequent Genshin Impact updates? Well, sit back, relax, and let us fill you in on everything you need to know. Mihoyo generally drops an update for the popular title every six weeks, meaning you never have long to wait for some fresh content.


The latest Genshin Impact update is version 1.5 and introduces the Serenitea Pot, Yanfei, Eula, much more. Read on below for full details of Genshin Impact update 1.5, including more information on each of the new characters, events, challenges, and enemies.

BENEATH THE LIGHT OF JADEITE

Moonlight falls on the branches as a dragon's roar rises from deep beneath the roots. A drop of sun may trickle in, but a heart of stone is not easily moved. Deals are sealed with scarlet flames and ice-cold vengeance floods forth in waves. But far from the fight is a realm of serenity, where a gentle melody forever plays.








GENSHIN IMPACT SERENITEA POT

Update 1.5 includes a brand new realm that Traveler can call their own. You access the new area with Genshin Impact’s Serenitea Pot, a special item that summons what looks like a penguin in a teapot.

In this special realm, you can build and decorate your home however you see fit. Numerous house and realm styles will be available, each with indoor and outdoor space for you to mould however you wish.

VERSION 1.5 ENEMIES

Two brand new enemies can also be found in update 1.5. Azhdaha, the ‘Lord of Vishaps,’ offers rare rewards once a week for anyone who manages to take her down. The Cryo Hypostasis will also make an appearance in Dragonspine as a new boss enemy.








CHALLENGE THE TWISTED REALM


The "Energy Amplifier Initiation" event is about to begin!
During the event, points can be acquired by challenging the Twisted Realm. Complete corresponding Domain challenges to unlock the Twisted Realm.



1.5 WISH BANNER

Zhongli’s banner has returned with Genshin Impact update 1.5! He is joined by Yanfei, Diona, and Noelle.





VERSION 1.5 RELEASE DATE

Genshin Impact 1.5 is out right now! We learned about the release date, and the following details, during a Genshin Impact update 1.5 livestream, which took place on April 16 at 13:00 GMT.
